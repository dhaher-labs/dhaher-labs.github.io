### <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/WUlplcMpOCEmTGBtBW/giphy.gif" width="40" align="left" /> About Me

By day, I maintain industrial systems at a cement packing plant — electrical, mechanical, control panels. By night, I build AI tools, quantitative research systems, and open-source software. Self-taught developer studying Information Systems at Universitas Terbuka.

Currently building **Dhaher Labs** — a small lab for practical AI and quantitative intelligence.

- 🏢 Check out [**Dhaher Labs**](https://github.com/dhaher-labs) — [**Website**](https://dhaher-labs.github.io)

- 💬 Ask me about: AI systems, LLM integration, industrial automation
- 📫 Reach me: [Email](mailto:mulkymalikuldhaher@mail.com) · [Instagram](https://instagram.com/mulkymalikuldhr)
